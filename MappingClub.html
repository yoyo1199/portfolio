Mapping Club

At my school I co-founded a club called mapping club. i was introduced to mapping through my expirences with Peace Corps.

Mapping is an activity on the computer that helps developing countries. Mapping uses satalite images providedby Open Street Map and OSM Tasking Manager to help map out roads and building in developing countries without a full GPS data.

After students complete an area, Peace Corps sends volenteers there to bring aid and assistance. Peace corps voleenteers spend 1 to 2 years teaching local people new ways to survive.

Peace Corps volenteers sometimes build thing thats will help provide clean water, or they will teach them new ways of farming.
